Just a KL girl

What do acquiring a foreign language and learning to code have in common? It started out with a lot of fun and feeling cool to be able to show off a new skill. This phase follow shortly by what one would describe as 'nothing makes sense and no breakthrough being made irregardless of the hours spent to push forward!

To be able to speak, eat, sing, dream and think in French was the reason I came to France. While learning la langue de Molière, I developed a passion for web development. I enrolled on the Web Development path on OpenClassroom with the plan to make a career out of my passion. 14 months down the line, a Web Development diploma in my toolbox, I am still as motivated as ever to continue learning. If that doesn't show commitment, I don't know what will.
